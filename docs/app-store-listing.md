## Description
FreshLocal connects you with talented local halal food makers in your neighbourhood. From authentic biryanis and curries to fresh-baked naan and sweet treats — discover homemade meals prepared with love, right around the corner.

**How it works:**
1. Browse meals from verified local vendors near you
2. Choose collection or delivery
3. Pick your time slot and pay securely
4. Enjoy fresh, homemade halal food

**For food lovers:**
- Discover hidden gems — talented home cooks and small food businesses
- Filter by cuisine: Pakistani, Bangladeshi, Indian, Middle Eastern & more
- Real-time order tracking from kitchen to your door
- Save your favourites for quick reordering
- Rate and review to help the community

**For food makers:**
- Turn your kitchen into a business
- Set your own menu, prices, and schedule
- Manage orders with our easy vendor dashboard
- Get paid weekly via Stripe — it's that simple
- Build a loyal customer base in your area

**Why FreshLocal?**
We believe the best food comes from passionate people, not factory kitchens. FreshLocal is built to support local halal food entrepreneurs — whether you're a home cook, a small shop, or a popup — while giving customers access to authentic, freshly made meals they won't find anywhere else.

All vendors are verified. All food is halal. All payments are secure.
